## How New Relic is Involved

In June 2021, New Relic donated Pixie to the [Cloud Native Computing Foundation (CNCF)](https://www.cncf.io/). Pixie is a CNCF [Sandbox project](https://www.cncf.io/sandbox-projects/). New Relic is a CNCF [Platinum Member](https://www.cncf.io/about/members/).
